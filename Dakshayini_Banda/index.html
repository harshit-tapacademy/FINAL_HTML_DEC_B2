1. Mr. Perfect

About:

Mr. Perfect is a 2011 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Dasaradh, and produced by Dil Raju under Sri Venkateswara Creations. The film features Prabhas, Kajal and Taapsee alongside Murali Mohan, Prakash Raj, Nassar, Sayaji Shinde and K. Vishwanath. The music is composed by Devi Sri Prasad.

2. Majili

About:

Majili (2019) is a Telugu romantic drama that follows the journey of Poorna, a talented cricketer whose career and life take unexpected turns after heartbreak. Haunted by his past love, he struggles with failure, alcohol, and lost dreams.

3. Raja Rani

About:

Raja Rani (2013) is a Tamil romantic drama that explores love, loss, and healing through an arranged marriage between John and Regina, two individuals carrying emotional scars from their past. As they slowly open up, their hidden love stories and heartbreaks are revealed.
